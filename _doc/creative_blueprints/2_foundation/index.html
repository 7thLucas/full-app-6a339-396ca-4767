The Problem

Most grocery apps show one store.
The best deal is somewhere else.

Consumers waste hours hunting prices across stores, missing promotions, and guessing which store gives the best overall value. No single app brings it all together — until now.

The Solution

One list. Every store. Best price found.

Add your grocery list by typing, photo, or voice. SmartCart AI scans real-time prices and promotions across every participating store within your radius, calculates the total at each, and tells you exactly where to go — or orders it for you.
