The Problem

Most health apps collect data.
They don't change behavior.

People track calories, steps, and sleep — then quit after two weeks. The problem isn't information; it's accountability. Without a consistent check-in, motivation evaporates and routines collapse.

The Solution

A daily coach in your pocket.

Personalized goal-setting, daily check-in prompts, streak tracking, and smart nudges — the accountability loop people actually need to make habits stick, delivered simply and consistently every day.
